Quick Rules

  • New paragraph every time a new person speaks.
  • If the same speaker continues, keep it in the same paragraph.
  • Use quotation marks around spoken words.
  • Punctuation goes inside the quotation marks (e.g., commas, full stops, question marks).
  • Use dialogue tags (said, asked, replied) or actions to show who is speaking.
